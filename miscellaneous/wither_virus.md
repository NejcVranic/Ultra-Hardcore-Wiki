# Wither Virus
When a player is hurt while is wither skeleton is within 3 blocks they will become a carrier of the wither virus.

When a player carrying the Wither Virus stands over a grass block or farmland the block will become a dormant carrier and the player will lose the virus. Eventually, the dormant carrier block will turn into a [Withered Soil](blocks/withered_soil.md) block or dirt.
