Ultra Hardcore adds 2 new utility blocks:

A forge has a 3x3 storage as well as an invisible item frame at the top of the block, referred to as the "surface slot". When there is an item in the surface slot, right clicking it will drop the item. If a block is placed above the forge will also cause the item in the surface slot to drop.

A forge must be powered for any of its usages to be enabled. (Note that each type of forge has different conditions to be powered).

A forge is active when powered and executing a process. While active, a forge will emit ash particles from the top of the block. A forge will stay active for 5 seconds before completing its running process.

### Usage
A forge block can be used to forge new items when the following conditions are met
- a valid forging recipe is used in its UI
- It's surface slot is empty

Armor and Tools can be repaired using the forge by
- inserting the armor or tool in the surface slot,
- placing an appropriate material in the top left or middle cell inside its 3x3 UI,
- and leaving the other cells empty

While a forge is being used to forge new items or repair existing items it is considered active and will remain active for a few seconds until the process is complete, after which a sound is played.
