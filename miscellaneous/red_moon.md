# Red Moon
Red Moon is a part of the [mob](../mobs.md) enhancement system. Every full moon (occurring on the first night and every eighth night) is a Red Moon.

During a Red Moon, each player’s Difficulty Score is temporarily increased by 2 while they are in the overworld.

Additional effects during a Red Moon:
- **Creepers** receive extra enhancements
- **Zombie Brutes** spawn more frequently (their replacement rate increases from _a%_ to _b%_)
- **Mob variants** have a higher chance of spawning
- Chance for **Zoglin** to replace spider spawn
- Killing mobs grants an additional **+5 XP**
