# Copper Tier
Copper tier is a [material tier](material_tiers/list.md), that refers to the collection of tool and armor piece [items](items.md) made from Copper Ingots.
It is stronger than [Flint Tier](material_tiers/flint_tier.md) and weaker than [Iron Tier](material_tiers/iron_tier).
> Currently (in Ultra Hardcore version 1) copper armor does not exist in the game.
> The tools are based on the Vanilla wooden tools and share their properties, except for durability.

The datapack adds the standard set of tools and the Copper shield.

The durability of all copper tools is 50, except for Gardenind shears which have 64 durability.

The Copper Pickaxe cannot be used to mine stone, andesite, granite, diorite, deepslate and deepslate ores.

The attack damage of the Iron Sword is 4 (equivalent to Vanilla Minecraft Wooden Sword).
