# Amethyst Tier
Amethyst tier is a [material tier](material_tiers/list.md), that refers to the collection of tool [items](items.md) made from blocks of amethyst.
It is the same strength as [Steel Tier](material_tiers/steel_tier).
> The amethyst tools in Ultra Hardcore are actually retextured iron tools.

All Amethyst tools are enchanted with Fortune 2 or Looting 2 when crafted.
