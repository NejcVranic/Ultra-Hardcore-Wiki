# Blaze Gold Tier
Blaze Gold tier is a [material tier](material_tiers/list.md), that refers to the collection of tool and armor piece [items](items.md) made from [Blaze Gold Ingots](items/blaze_gold_ingot).
It is stronger than [Steel Tier](material_tiers/steel_tier) and weaker than [Diamond Tier](material_tiers/diamond_tier).
> The blaze gold armor & tools in Ultra Hardcore are actually retextured golden armor & tools.

The items can only be obtained by [forging](miscellaneous/forges) them in the [Deepslate Forge](blocks/deepslate_forge) or [Nether Forge](blocks/nether_forge).

While wearing the full armor set, the player gains **fire resistance**.

The items will **repair** themselves when the player is on fire while wearing the armor or holding the tools in their main hand or offhand. The items cannot be repaired in a forge.

The items can be upgraded to [diamond tier](material_tiers/diamond_tier) in the [Nether Forge](blocks/nether_forge). If the blaze gold item has any enchantments, the upgraded diamond item will have them as well. 

The attack damage of the Blaze Gold Sword is 7 (equivalent to Diamond Sword in Vanilla Minecraft).
