# Diamond Tier
Diamond tier is a [material tier](../material_tiers/list.md), that refers to the collection of tool and armor piece [items](../items.md) made from [Diamond Ingots](../items/diamond_ingot.md).
It is stronger than [Blaze Gold Tier](../material_tiers/blaze_gold_tier.md) and weaker than [Netherite Tier](../material_tiers/netherite_tier.md).

The items can (only) be obtained by upgrading a [blaze gold item](../material_tiers/blaze_gold_tier.md) in the [Nether Forge](../blocks/nether_forge.md).

The items can also be repaired in a forge by placing them in the surface slot and putting a diamond inside the forge.

The items can be upgraded to [netherite tier](../material_tiers/netherite_tier.md) in the [Nether Forge](../blocks/nether_forge.md). If the diamond item has any enchantments, the upgraded netherite item will have them as well. 

The attack speed of the Diamond Sword has been increased from 1.6 to 1.2.
