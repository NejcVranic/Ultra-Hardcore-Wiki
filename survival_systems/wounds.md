# Wounds
Wounds are a [Survival System](survival_systems.md) which introduces lasting injuries and additional consequences to taking damage.

Whenever a player takes damage, there is a **1 in 5 chance** they become **Wounded**. A wounded player’s status is displayed above the hotbar. While wounded, the player emits red particle effects when hurt and may take random damage while sprinting.

If a wounded player takes damage again, there is a **1 in 5 chance** they become **Severely Wounded**. Severely wounded players will take damage every few seconds.

Players can recover from wounds by gaining the **Regeneration** effect. In the early game, it is most easily obtained by using a [Bandage](items/bandage).

### Broken legs
Falling from sufficient height will cause the player to **break their leg**, reducing movement speed.

The fall distance required depends on the level of **Feather Falling** on the player’s boots:
- No Feather Falling: ~3 blocks
- Feather Falling I: ~4 blocks
- Feather Falling II: ~5 blocks
- Feather Falling III: ~6 blocks
- Feather Falling IV: ~7 blocks
- Feather Falling V: Cannot break leg

### Infections
If a player is **poisoned while wounded**, their wounds become **Infected**. Infected players cannot recover from the poison effect.

Because poison also increases the likelihood of becoming severely wounded, it is extremely dangerous and often fatal if not treated quickly.
