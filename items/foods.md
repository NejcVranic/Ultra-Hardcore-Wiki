### More info

Apple Pie
- Recipe (shapeless): apple + egg + sugar

Cactus Soup
- Recipe (shapeless): 2 cactus + sugar + bowl

Diamond Apple
- Recipe: surround apple with 8 diamonds 
- Gives Regeneration 4 effect for 5 seconds and another 5 seconds of Regeneration 2 + same effect as eating golden apple
- Immediately heals all wounds
- Found in Stronghold loot chests

Emerald Apple
- Recipe: surround apple with 8 diamonds 
- Gives Luck 1 effect for 2 minutes + same effect as eating golden apple
- Found in Stronghold loot chests

Glazed Ham
- Recipe (shapeless): Porkchop + Honey bottle

Glow Berry Pie
- Recipe (shapeless): 2 Glow berry + egg + sugar

Glow Squid Stew
- Recipe (shapeless): (Glow) squid tentacle or Tropical fish + Glow Inq sac + dried kelp + bowl

Goulash
- Recipe (shapeless): Steak or Porkchop + Baked potato + Carrot + bowl  

Hot cocoa
- Recipe (shapeless): Milk bucket + cocoa + sugar + bowl
- ALWAYS adds 2 to micronutrients score, regardless of other factors

Mango
- Can be obtained by decaying or breaking jungle leaves 
- Is a retextured baked potato and can be used instead of it in crafting recipes

Meat Pie
- Recipe (shapeless): Steak + egg + wheat

Rabbit Stew
- Recipe (shapeless): Cooked rabbit + Baked potato + Carrot + Red or Brown mushroom + bowl

Squid Stew
- Recipe (shapeless): Squid tentacle or Glow squid tentacle or Tropical fish + Inq sac + Dried kelp + bowl

Sweet Berry Pie
- Recipe (shapeless): 2 sweet berry + egg + sugar

Squid tentacle
- Dropped by Squid

Glow Squid Tentacle
- Dropped by Glowing squid

Glow Squid Stew
- Recipe (shapeless): Squid tentacle or Glow squid tentacle or Tropical fish + Glow inq sac + Dried kelp + bowl
