# Filtered Water Bottle
Filtered Water Bottle is an [item](../items.md) that can be drank to recover hydration. 

It gives 2000 [Hydration](../hydration.md) when consumed (fills 1/3 of the hydration bar).

### Obtaining
A Water Bottle will turn into a Filtered water bottle if left as an item on a campfire for 30 seconds, after which it will start emitting bubbling sounds.
