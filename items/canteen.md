# Canteen
Canteen is a tool [item](items.md) that can hold water and, unlike a water bottle, can be drank from multiple times. 

<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; gap: 24px;">  
<div style="flex: 1;">  
<p>Drinking from a canteen that has not been filled with clean (boiled) water carries a high chance of applying negative effects such as Hunger or Poison, similar to drinking from a regular water bottle.</p>
<p>The canteen’s durability bar indicates how many uses remain. The item does not break when durability reaches zero and can be refilled. Each refill restores the canteen to 10 uses.</p>  
</div>

### Usage
Holding use (right click) will start a drinking sound and animation. When consumed the water restores 2000 hydration.

Canteen can be refilled with water at any time by right-clicking on a water source or a water-filled cauldron. This will fill the canteen, consuming the water in the same way as a bucket.

A canteen can be filled with **clean water** under the following conditions:
- Water is collected from a source block with a **magma block** beneath it
- Water is taken from a cauldron with a **fire** or **campfire** below
- The canteen is placed on a **lit campfire** and left until it begins to produce bubbling sounds

If none of these conditions are met, the canteen water is considered **unclean** and is unsafe to drink.
