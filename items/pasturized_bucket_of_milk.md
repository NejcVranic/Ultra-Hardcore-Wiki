# Pasturized Bucket of Milk
Pasturized Bucket of Milk is a version of the Bucket of Milk [item](items.md) that can be drank to recover hydration. Unlike the regular Bucket of Milk, it is safe to drink and will never cause adverse effects.

It gives 2000 [Hydration](hydration.md) when consumed (fills 1/3 of the hydration bar).

### Obtaining
A Bucket of Milk will turn into a Pasturized Bucket of Milk if left as an item on a campfire for 30 seconds, after which it will start emitting bubbling sounds.
