# Gardening Shears
Gardening Shears is a tool [item](../items.md) that can be used to remove weeds from [Poor Farmland](../blocks/poor_farmland.md).

<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; gap: 24px;">  
<div style="flex: 1;">  
<p>When used on poor farmland with weeds growing on the the block, the weeds will be removed and oak leaves will drop.</p>
</div>
