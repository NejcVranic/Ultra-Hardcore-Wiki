# Survival Systems 
Ultra Hardcore introduces multiple new systems that make it a challenge to survive.
Each system can afflict the player with negative effects, which can stack. Some may also grant bonuses to the player under the right conditions.

List of survival systems:
- [Cold](cold.md) - Adds conditions for freezing
- [Health Scaling](health_scaling.md) - Changes how many hearts a player has
- [Hydration](hydration.md) - Adds a hydration bar
- [Nutrition](nutrition.md) - Tracks the quality of a player's diet and its impact on their health
- [Sanity](sanity.md) - Adds stages of insanity
- [Wounds](wounds.md) - Adds wound and bleeding
