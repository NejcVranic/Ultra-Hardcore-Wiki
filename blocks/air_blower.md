# Air Blower
Air Blower is a [block](blocks.md) that can be used to power the [Nether Forge](blocks/nether_forge) and as decoration.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; gap: 24px;">  
<div style="flex: 1;">  
<p>When placed the block will face in the player's direction.</p>
<p>If a piston head is extended behind the block (side with leather texture) it will attempt to fan a flame in front of it (opposite side with a hole). When the flame is fanned it will remain fanned for a short duration and emit tall lava particles.</p>  
</div>
