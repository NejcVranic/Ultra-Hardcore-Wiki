# Snare Trap
Snare Trap is a [block](../blocks.md) that can be used to catch wild Rabbits in the forest biome.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; gap: 24px;">  
<div style="flex: 1;">  
<p>The block appears as a closed oak trapdoor, with a leaf texture over the top.</p>
<p>When placed in the forest biome, opened and surrounded by 4 grass blocks (north, south, west and east), an attempt to spawn a Rabbit will be made every 2.5 seconds. When succesfull, the block will be replaced with a closed oak trapdoor and a Rabbit will spawn in the trapdoor. (No Rabbits need to be nearby for this to succeed.)</p>
</div>
