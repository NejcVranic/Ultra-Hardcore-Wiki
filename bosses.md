# Bosses

# Ender Dragon
The Ender Dragon now attacks the player with dragon fireballs more frequently. When dragon fireballs land, they also explode. The dragon can also spawn large phantoms and can spawn portals while pearched.

A portal is a small structure with an end crystal on top. It heals the Ender Dragon and summon up to 6 mobs from the other dimensions. There are 2 types of portals: Overworld (summon only overworld mobs) and Nether (summon only nether mobs). If the end crystal is destroyed, the portal is destroyed also.

As the dragon loses health the fight become more difficult:

190+ health points
- No added attack

70 to 190 health points
- Random chance to summon a phantom
- When pearched the dragon will open up to 3 portals

70- health points
- When pearched the dragon will open up to 6 portals
- The dragon can shoot the player, when it is above Y height 75. A barrage of 5 dragon fireballs is fired, aimed at the player's location.

# Wither
The Wither's health is increased from 300 to 500 health points.

While a player is nearby the Wither can start and stop spawning Wither Skeletons at random. The Wither skeletons spawned this way do not have a Stone Sword.
